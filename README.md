### 👋 About

I build systems that sit between software and the real world — from ML pipelines and analytics dashboards to embedded hardware.

I'm a Computer Science (Data Science) undergraduate at **Dayananda Sagar University**, currently maintaining a **9.09 CGPA**. My work spans two tracks: **AI/ML & data analytics** (predictive models, SQL/Power BI dashboards, EDA pipelines) and **embedded/IoT engineering** (sensor-driven automation on ESP32). I also co-authored a research paper on AI-driven environmental control, currently under review at InnovateX 2025.

Right now I'm building an early-warning forest fire prediction system for Uttarakhand, and preparing for AI/ML and Data Science internships through hands-on projects and continuous learning.

### 📄 Research

**AI-Powered Smart Window Ventilation & Environmental Control** — *InnovateX 2025 (Under Review)*
Co-authored a multi-sensor IoT system (ESP32, AHT10, PIR, LDR, YL-83) driven by a rule-based AI engine computing a Comfort Deviation Index (CDI) for automated window control, paired with a real-time Flask + Chart.js dashboard — achieving a 31% reduction in ventilation energy use and a 27% improvement in occupant comfort.
